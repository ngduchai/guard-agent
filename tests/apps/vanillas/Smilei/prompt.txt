I want you to make this MPI application resilient against mid-execution
process failures, using the VeloC library which is already installed on
the system.

Throughout your work, narrate your reasoning out loud around every
action you take — internal reasoning, reading files, editing files,
running commands, anything else. There are no exceptions.

Before each action, state:
  - what you intend to do,
  - why you are about to do it (the motivation or assumption that
    prompted it).
  - what is your expectation of the action

After each action, state:
  - what the result was,
  - how that result confirms or contradicts the motivation you had
    beforehand.

If you encounter any issue or failure, before applying any fix:
  1. Quote the exact error message you are reacting to.
  2. State your hypothesis for the root cause.
  3. Describe the specific change you intend to make and why it
     should fix it.
Then make the change.
