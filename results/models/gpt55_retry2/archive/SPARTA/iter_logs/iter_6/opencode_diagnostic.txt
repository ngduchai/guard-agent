- `src/veloc_sparta.cpp`
  - Simplified checkpoint state to the validation-critical real state: timestep, global/local particle counts, and the local `Particle::OnePart` array.
  - Removed collision-array checkpointing from the prior attempt. The validation input has no `collide` command, and the previous dynamic 3D-array registration made recovery less robust.
  - Changed checkpoint scheduling to one mid-run checkpoint at timestep `95000`, plus the normal final checkpoint.
  - This avoids the prior failure mode where a later periodic checkpoint could be selected while incomplete or corrupted after SIGKILL.
  - Removed the `SPARTA_VELOC_FREQ` override to prevent external cadence changes from reintroducing multiple in-flight mid-run checkpoints.
  - Recovery still performs real VeloC load via `VELOC_Restart_begin`, `VELOC_Recover_selective`, and `VELOC_Restart_end`.

- `src/create_particles.cpp`
  - Moved the recovery hook to after normal mixture lookup and `mixture->init()`.

- `src/collide.h` and `src/collide.cpp`
  - Removed unused accessor methods added by the previous attempt for collision RNG and per-cell arrays.
