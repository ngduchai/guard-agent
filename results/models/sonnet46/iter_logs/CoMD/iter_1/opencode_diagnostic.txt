**Key design choices:**
- The checkpoint version number is `iStep` (the step after the completed batch), which monotonically increases — satisfying VeloC's requirement that version numbers increase
- All atom arrays are registered at their full allocated size (`MAXATOMS * nTotalBoxes`) — this captures the link-cell-indexed layout faithfully
- On restart, `iStep` is restored from the checkpoint, so `for (; iStep<nSteps;)` continues correctly from the restored step
- The `VELOC_Restart_test` second argument `0` means "find latest version < 0" which in VeloC semantics means "find the latest available" (version 0 = no upper bound constraint)
