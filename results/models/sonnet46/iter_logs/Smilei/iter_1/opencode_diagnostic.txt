The two paths are distinct absolute `/tmp` paths as required by the validator.

**2. Modified `src/Smilei.cpp`**:

- Added `#include <cstdint>` and `#include <veloc.h>`

- Added two helper functions **before** `main()`:
  - `veloc_write_state()`: called within `VELOC_Checkpoint_begin/end`. Gets a routed file path via `VELOC_Route_file`, then writes: iteration number, patch count, 6 EM field arrays (Ex/Ey/Ez/Bx/By/Bz) per patch with size+data, and particle arrays (Position, Momentum×3, Weight, Charge) per species per patch.
  - `veloc_read_state()`: called within `VELOC_Restart_begin/end`. Reads the same format back, restoring field data and particle arrays in-place.

- **`VELOC_Init(smpi.world(), "veloc.cfg")`** is called immediately after MPI initialization.

- **Restart check** before the PIC loop: calls `VELOC_Restart_test(name, INT_MAX)` to find the latest checkpoint. If found, calls `VELOC_Restart_begin/veloc_read_state/VELOC_Restart_end` and sets `restart_itime`, `time_prim`, and `time_dual` accordingly. The loop starts at `restart_itime+1`.

- **Checkpoint every 100 iterations** (outside the OpenMP parallel region, after all physics and diagnostics): calls `VELOC_Checkpoint_begin/veloc_write_state/VELOC_Checkpoint_end`.

- **`VELOC_Finalize(0)`** called at both the normal exit and test-mode exit paths, before `MPI_Finalize` (which happens in the `SmileiMPI` destructor).
