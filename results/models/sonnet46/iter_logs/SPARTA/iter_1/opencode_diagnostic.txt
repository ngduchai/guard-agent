**Restart** (at the top of each `run()` call):

- Computes `istart = saved_ntimestep - firststep` to skip already-completed iterations

The design correctly handles multi-`run` input scripts: each `run()` invocation queries only for checkpoints within its own time range, so earlier runs are fast-forwarded and the critical run resumes from the right step.
