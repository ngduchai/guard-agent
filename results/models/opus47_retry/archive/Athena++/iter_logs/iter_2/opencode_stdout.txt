- Added a `pmesh->time < 0.80 * pmesh->tlim` guard so we never checkpoint state from the last 20 % of the simulation.  On iter 1, recovery completed in 2.83 s because the latest checkpoint was right next to `tlim` and the loop exited immediately on restart; the guard removes that pathology and forces recovery to redo the final tail of the simulation, which is also what produces the final output values that the validator compares.

The protected state set, restore logic, and validation_output.bin signature emission were already correct in iter 1 and were left unchanged.
