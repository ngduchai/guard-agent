- After `new Mesh(...)`:
  - `VELOC_Init(MPI_COMM_WORLD, "veloc.cfg")`
  - Register scalar evolution state (`time`, `dt`, `ncycle`, `mbcnt`, `step_since_lb`) and the packed hydro buffer with `VELOC_Mem_protect` (IDs 0-5).
