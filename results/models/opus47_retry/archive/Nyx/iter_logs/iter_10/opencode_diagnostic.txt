## Fix

AMReX will allocate it in `swapTimeLevels()` before any read on the next coarseTimeStep, so the resumed state stays correct.

I also added `ParallelDescriptor::Barrier` + stderr log markers (`PRE-INIT`, `POST-INIT`, `SCATTER lev=0 begin/done`, `BOOKKEEP lev=0 done`, `RESUME complete`) around each resume step so any future failure region appears in the validator's stderr tail.
