   - `NyxVelocMeta` POD struct holding `has_state`, `n_levels`, `cum_time`, `level_steps[]`, `level_count[]`, `dt_level[]`, `buf_size[]`.
   - Helpers `nyx_mf_to_buffer` / `nyx_buffer_to_mf` to (de)serialize a `MultiFab`'s valid-cell data via `MFIter` + `Array4` indexing.
   - On startup: `VELOC_Init(MPI_COMM_WORLD, "veloc.cfg")`, register meta, `VELOC_Restart_test/begin`, recover meta-only (id 0).
   - After `Amr::init()`: allocate per-level state buffers sized from the meta, register them with stable ids (`1 + L*NSTATES + S`), `VELOC_Recover_selective` for them, scatter into `State_Type` MultiFabs, copy new→old data, then `setCumTime/setLevelSteps/setLevelCount/setDtLevel` so the coarse-time-step loop resumes from the right point.
   - Each iteration of the time loop: pack every level's `State_Type` MultiFab into its buffer, refresh `VELOC_Mem_protect` (vector may have reallocated), update meta header, call `VELOC_Checkpoint("nyx", ++version)`.
   - At shutdown: `VELOC_Finalize(0)`.

The restart relies on level 0's BoxArray being deterministic across runs (fixed by `amr.n_cell`/`amr.max_grid_size`). Higher levels are restored where their layouts happen to match, otherwise the level is skipped and regenerated by subsequent AMReX regrids.
