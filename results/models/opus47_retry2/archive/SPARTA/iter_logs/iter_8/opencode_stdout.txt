- At checkpoint time: compute checksum, register it as a 5th protected region (6 doubles), and write it alongside the other state.
- At restore time: read the saved checksum, recompute it from the just-restored particle buffer, MPI_Allreduce(MIN) the per-rank equality flag. If any rank's bytes don't match, reject the version via `Restart_end(0)` + `Mem_clear` and walk down to an older checkpoint via the existing `probe_max_local = ckpt_v - 1` mechanism.
- Defensive: memset the entire allocated `particle->particles` buffer (up to `maxlocal`, not just `derived_nlocal`) before recovery so stale data from the fresh `create_particles` call can't leak into later migration receive slots.
- Diagnostic stderr logs for both CKPT and RESTORE-CHECKSUM events.
