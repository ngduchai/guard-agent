- **Init**: `VELOC_Init(MPI_COMM_WORLD, "veloc.cfg")` uses collective mode with all MPI ranks
- **Protect**: Registers two scalars (`veloc_iter`: int, `veloc_waqt`: double) and all simulation solution arrays (`sim[ns].solver.u`) using `VELOC_Mem_protect`

- **Finalize**: `VELOC_Finalize(0)` after the loop
