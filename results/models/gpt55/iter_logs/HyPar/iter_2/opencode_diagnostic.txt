  - Registers `TS.iter` and `TS.waqt`.
  - Registers each actual `sim[ns].solver.u` solution array, instead of `TS.u`.
  - Restarts directly into the real solver solution arrays.
  - Checkpoints every `n_iter / 20` iterations plus the final iteration, avoiding millions of sync checkpoints while still giving the validator a useful restart point.
